About Me

I'm a 23 year old Library and Information Science student with an interest in User Experience Research and Writing. I'm passionate about exploring how people form user communities according to their unique information needs. I'm excited about making the internet and mobile technologies more accessible, usable, and approachable. A Bay Area native, I graduated from UC Santa Barbara in 2014 with a Bachelors Degree in English and expect to graduate from San Jose State University in December 2016 with a Masters in Library and Information Science.

In my spare time I enjoy reading comics and graphic novels, creating seasonal mixtapes, and exploring every kind of breakfast food. My productivity is fueled by a combination of pop culture podcasts and matcha shots. At any given time I can be found at an assortment of coffee shops up and down the Bay Area.
